Investment Thesis & Methodology

Federal Reserve tightening cycle vs. Economic normalization

We analyze valuation de-ratings since the monetary tightening period. As global rates normalize, we look for **re-rating opportunities**—assets trading cheap relative to their own history but backed by stable profitability.

We define:
• Strong Re-rating Candidate: Trading cheap ($PE \le -1\text{SD}$) with stable or improving profitability ($\Delta ROE \ge -2\%$).
• Potential Value Trap: Trading cheap ($PE \le -1\text{SD}$) but profitability has collapsed ($\Delta ROE < -5\%$).
• Overvalued / Neutral: Trading above historical average.
